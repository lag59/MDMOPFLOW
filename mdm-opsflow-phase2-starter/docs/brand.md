# MDM OpsFlow Brand

**Founder:** Libia A. Gaviria, RN, BSN  
**Positioning:** The AI Operating System for Construction  
**Audience:** Small companies, contractors, growing organizations, and industry leaders.

## Mission
Empower construction businesses of every size through AI-powered automation, connected operations, and real-time intelligence.

## Promise
Enter data once. Let AI do the rest.

## Taglines
- The AI Operating System for Construction.
- Build smarter. Powered by AI.
- Construction intelligence, connected.
- From estimate to closeout.

## Logo Guidance
- Primary lockup: MDM OpsFlow full-color mark on light backgrounds.
- Dark-surface variant: use full-color or white-monochrome mark for navy surfaces.
- Monochrome variant: reserve for constrained placements (print, stamps, utility labels).
- Clear space: keep at least the height of the "M" around the mark.
- Do not stretch, recolor, rotate, or place over busy imagery without contrast backing.

## Typography
- Headings and UI: Inter
- Data and numeric content: JetBrains Mono
- Tone: clean, readable, modern, high-contrast

## Illustration and Photography
- Illustration style: simple line art with blueprint influence.
- Icon style: clean rounded outline icons with consistent stroke weight.
- Photography style: real field operations, equipment, people, and jobsite context.

## UI Direction
- Overall style: modern, premium, professional, calm.
- Layout style: spacious cards, strong hierarchy, reduced clutter.
- Interaction style: minimal clicks, inline edits, smart defaults, clear recovery states.
- AI surfaces: use blue accents and confidence indicators with visible reasoning metadata.

## Bilingual Requirements
- English and Spanish are first-class in all screens and components.
- Support longer Spanish strings without clipping or layout collapse.
- Keep user-level language preference and company default language.
- Provide instant language switching in navigation and settings.

## Accessibility
- Ensure sufficient color contrast in light and dark themes.
- Preserve focus visibility for all interactive controls.
- Maintain keyboard navigation across dialogs, tables, and form flows.
